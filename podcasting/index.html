Podcasting

Combining my knowledge of software development and audio engineering, I started creating podcasts in the earliest days of the medium back in 2003. Since that time, I have published hundreds of episodes across a half dozen podcasts.

Background

As a lifetime content creator, I began experimenting with online media syndication in 2003 while teaching myself code during audio engineering school.

Role

I am typically involved with every part of the production process; from ideation, hosting, production, design, syndication, and online marketing.

Goal

Every person and every initiative has a story to tell. Episodic media publishing has proven an extremely effective way to communicate to drive engagement.

Outcome

Though the years, I've published hundreds of episodes across a half dozen podcasts and continue to help content creators by building production utilities through services like Congo (formerly podcast.io).
